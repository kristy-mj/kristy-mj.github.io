Week One

March 17, 2016

Coming into EDA I don't know much about web development and have minimal experience writing code. I come from a background in textile design and my experience with computers has predominantely been using design software in the creation of visual elements. I am aware of my inexperience and can easily say I don't know what I am doing. This unknowing about what I am getting into is a bit scary.

Working through the first week at EDA this concern has lessened and developed more into an excitement at the prospect of learning much more. Initially I was worried I wouldn't know enough to keep up however as I have worked through the weeks tasks, both struggling with and solving issues, I have found it is at a level I am able to understand and I'm beginning to really enjoy the challenges.

Even though everything has just been online so far, I have found the culture EDA aims to cultivate within each cohort really supportive and it looks like this could be invaluable to the course.

At the moment, this is all still feeling fairly new however so far this sense of unknowing sits ok with me and I am really looking forward to the next few months.
