Control

Developing Self-Mastery

Chapter 5 of Search Inside Yourself looked at how to use self-awareness to master our emotions. This doesn't mean supressing emotions but being skillful in how we handle them or "making friends with our emotions". There were a few suggested methods that I would like to futher explore...

STOP-BREATHE-NOTICE-REFLECT-RESPOND. This idea of pausing, taking a moment to breathe and notice what's going on. I think it is super easy to be too hard on ourselves and feel bad about feeling bad so I like the idea of starting every thought with kindness and compassion for both oneself and others. Having humour and curiosity is another fun idea.

I found the mindfulness meditation this week really beneficial in gaining more clarity in my thoughts - although I do find I keep getting somewhat distracted and have to constantly bring my attention back to my breathing.
